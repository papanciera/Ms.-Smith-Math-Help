GRASSO TECH DISTANCE LEARNING HELP DESK

WE CAN DO THIS!

Grasso Tech may be closed, but our community is more than just a building. We can continue to learn together while we are socially distancing. Check this site for resources to help you. Above all, don't be shy about asking questions and reaching out for help. You are not alone.

GETTING STARTED

I know it can be difficult to concentrate when you're working remotely. Here are a few tips to help get started.

CREATE A WORKSPACE

It doesn't have to be anything fancy. My new "office" is a corner of my dining room table. When I'm here, I know I'm at work. Having a dedicated work space can really help you work at home.

LOG INTO GOOGLE CLASSROOM

If your teachers are planning group activities, they will happen during their assigned time slot. Check Google Classroom to see what your teachers have planned.

GET ORGANIZED

Make a plan for what you need to do each day. Cheek Google Classroom every morning to make sure you haven't forgotten anything. Your school agenda or a paper calendar can help you organize your day. Google Calendar, the Reminders App on your phone, or Google Keep are great organizational tools. Check out the videos below for ideas.

TAKE MOVEMENT AND BRAIN BREAKS

Sometimes staying focused requires taking a break. Get up and move around to get your blood moving. Set a timer to help you get back to work.

IT'S OK IF IT'S HARD SOMETIMES

We're all figuring this new way of learning out together. It's ok to make mistakes. Use the resources your teachers provide, ask questions, and do the best you can.

REACH OUT!

Communicating with your teachers and classmates will help you feel more connected. If you are having trouble, reach out to your teacher, guidance counselor, administrator, case manager, student services professional, or any trusted adult. We're here to help.
